Project Summary

I designed an entertainment stand to hold a 55-in TV and a PlayStation 5. It is made of wood and consists of 7 wooden boards, 30 wooden dowels, and 4 velvet furniture pads (to prevent the stand from damaging the floor). The top and bottom boards are identical in shape and size, and the 5 middle boards perpendicular to the top and bottom boards form 4 cubby holes which can be used for a variety of purposes.
