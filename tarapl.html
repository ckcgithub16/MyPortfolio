Targeting And Range-Automated Projectile Launcher
TAR-APL

I designed and constructed a projectile launching system that measures the distance to a target and adjusts the launch angle based on a measurement input. I used a Raspberry Pi as my micropressor and programmed in Python. I did this as part of a two-year high school internship program run by Northrop Grumman called the Northrop Grumman High School Involvement Partnership (HIP) program. I was assigned three mentors who were engineers at a local Northrop Grumman facilty who supported me during this project.
