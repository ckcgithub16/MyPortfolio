Low-Cost Multi-Patient Ventilator System (LCMPVS)


I worked to optimize, design, build, program, and rigorously test a low-cost ventilator system to remotely control multiple ventilators through a server. I used a Raspberry Pi as my micropressor and programmed in Java. I did this as part of a two-year high school internship program run by Northrop Grumman called the Northrop Grumman High School Involvement Partnership (HIP) program. I was assigned three mentors who were engineers at a local Northrop Grumman facilty who supported me during this project.
